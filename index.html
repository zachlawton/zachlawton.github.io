Introduction


HOWDY! My name is Zachary Lawton, and I am a junior Computer Science major at Texas A&M University from Houston, TX. Going into college, I did not know what exactly I wanted to do with my life. I had many interests that could've led me to major in civil engineering, urban planning, history, or psychology. I finally chose computer science because it allows me to explore each of these passions to create software that can hopefully make a positive impact on people around the world.

Hobbies/Interests


When I am not doing schoolwork, I keep myself occupied by participating in multiple hobbies. I enjoy working out at the gym and playing sports, specifically football and basketball. My favorite football team is the Green Bay Packers and my favorite basketball team is the Houston Rockets. I also love to travel! I have been to 23 states (plus D.C.) and one country overseas (Italy). When I am older, I want to travel around the world and immerse myself into different cultures. When I am not outside or travelling, I also enjoy playing both classical and modern pieces on alto saxophone, a hobby that I have had ever since middle school. I also enjoy going to try new food places, watching moves, and playing video games with my friends.
