About Me



Introduction

HOWDY! My name is Zachary Lawton, and I am a junior Computer Science major at Texas A&M University Going into college, I did not know what exactly I wanted to do with my life. I had many interests that could've led me to major in civil engineering, urban planning, history, or psychology. I finally chose computer science because it allows me to explore each of these passions to create software that can hopefully make a positive impact on people around the world.

Hobbies/Interests

When I am not doing schoolwork, I keep myself occupied by participating in multiple hobbies. I enjoy working out and playing sports, specifically football and basketball. My favorite football team is the Green Bay Packers and my favorite basketball team is the Houston Rockets. When I am inside, I also enjoy playing classical and modern pieces on alto saxophone, a hobby that I have had ever since middle school. I also enjoy watching movies and playing video games with friends.
